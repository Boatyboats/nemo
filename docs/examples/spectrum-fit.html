Spectrum Fit

We will use tabnllsqfit to subtract a bad baseline 
    tabplot spectrum1.tab line=1,1 yapp=1/xs
    tabnllsqfit spectrum1.tab xrange=-100:-20,45:150 fit=poly order=3 > spectrum1.par
    p0=$(txtpar spectrum1.par p0=p0=,1,2 p1=p1=,1,2 p2=p2=,1,2 p3=p3=,1,2)
    tabnllsqfit spectrum1.tab fit=poly order=3 out=- free=0,0,0,0 par="$p0" | tabcomment - > spectrum1fit.tab
    tabplot spectrum1fit.tab 1 3 -50 50  -1 5 line=1,1 ycoord=0  yapp=2/xs
